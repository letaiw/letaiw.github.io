Layan Etaiwi

  • Research Scientist, Software Engineer, Data Scientist & Procaffeinator ☕

I’m currently an Industrial Researcher at Siemens Advanced Experience Research, where I focus on data- and AI-driven research aimed at shaping design decisions in digital products, with the goal of delivering seamless, efficient, and user-centered experiences.

I received the Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Polytechnique Montréal, Canada in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Yann-Gaël Guéhéneuc and Prof. Sylvie Hamel. I am part of the Ptidej (Concordia University) and LBIT (Université de Montréal) research groups. I also collaborate with Centre for Artificial Intelligence (Zurich University of Applied Sciences) and Institute of Neuroinformatics (University of Zurich) reserach labs.

My research work includes Recommendation Systems, Machine Learning, Software Maintenance and Evolution, Software Architecture, Empirical Software Engineering, and User Experience.
